Professional Projects

DMSi Automated Warehouse Inventory

My Senior Design team was task with automating the inventory of a lumber warehouse. We proposed the solution of tracking indiviual lumber bundle movements. To accomplish this we placed QR codes on each bundle and a camera on each forklift. We then read the bundle information and compute a map of the warehouse for customer to use.

Husker-Net

Husker-Net is an open end-to-end cellular edge network. Husker-Net offers campus-wide wireless connectivity (5G) and edge computing capability, including City, East and Innovation Campus, and is freely open to researchers at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. It is my responsiblity to collect RSRP and location data to analyze.
